Blackjack on Kubernetes

A browser build of my Python blackjack game. The panel on the right mirrors the real MicroK8s deployment it runs on — deployment, ClusterIP service, nginx ingress with sticky sessions, and a horizontal pod autoscaler. Every action you take is logged as a request routed to the blackjack-dealer pods.
